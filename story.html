Our Story

Miguelito's started as an Idea. The idea that it doesn't matter where you're from or what is going on in your life, good mexican food can bring a smile to your face. The founder and owner of Miguelito's understood this idea, so he took it upon himself to create a restaurant that is capable of bring that joy and pleasure to anyone who walks into this slice of Mexico. With fresh cooked ingredients brought straight to your table, you'll feel like you are already in Mexico!
